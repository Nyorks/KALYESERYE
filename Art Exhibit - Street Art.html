What is Street Art?

A unique form of art that is only found in the streets. It typically occurs in metropolitan settings and is generated on surfaces in public locations, including sidewalks, overpasses, and outside building walls. Street art is a common medium for expressing artworks that are either connected to politics or social issues or are just for fun. These encompass a variety, such as "3D wall," "sidewalk chalk art," "graffiti art," "stencil graffiti," "sticker art," and "street poster art." Thus, not all street art consists of painting.

About Our Exhibit

Welcome to Kalyeserye!

In this exhibit the artists will show street art as something unique for us, because that is where the real purpose of an artist can be seen. It is also an initiative that highlights the ability of street art is to empower people to communicate, and promote conversation about topics that are relevant.

This exhibit, KALYESERYE: Pagbibigay Pansin sa Sining ng Kalye, will show the various rage of creativity based on their personal interest and imagination. We also share ideas and knowledge to accomplish this art exhibit. While we are creating our art works we are doing it passionately and giving our time and effort.

Here you can see the works of students that represents street art; structures, roads and buildings, graffiti, vandalism, and other street paintings. Each student in our group contributed and shared their talent in drawing, painting, sculpture, and photography, and even encoded a website to promote Philippine Street Art. We can say that we are happy to show you our creations. Thank you for visiting our website. MABUHAY!
